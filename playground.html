As intrinsic as eat and sleep.
Play is as intrinsic to us as eating and sleeping. It is a medium of forming connections, building trust and expressing ourselves. Art is an attempt by the universe to produce. To being an idea or a though to a tangible concrete and defined structure that can be shared. Art can influence, inspire, and fulfill the human desire to create. We engage in play out of our curiosity. A successful play is when the player enters out of their desire, and competes for a fair and equal set of rules. Art leaves behind your identity and proof of your existence. Create art not because you express it, but because it expresses you. Art can be expressed through any medium. It is an act of complex cognitive reasoning, almost all done subconsciously. To exist is to product.
